The Best Movies According to Irene

My top 3 movies of all-time

Little Miss Sunshine

Plot summary is misleading. Hilarious film about a dysfunctional family going on a journey with a big loss and realistic family bonding

The Grand Budapest Hotel

Visually stunning, funny story of drama and intrigue

Billy Elliot

Young boy wants to be a ballet dancer. Infuriates his abusive father. Great film.
